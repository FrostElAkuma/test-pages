How to get Drang (BAROQUE) with Incandecent roll

Method 1: From Opulant Chests


The Drang (BAROQUE) can drop randomly from opening Opulant chests in the Leviathan among other Opulant weapons.
The Incandecent Roll is rng but opening more chests increases the chance of you getting one.
In order to open an opulent chest you need an opulent key which can drop randoomly from opening chests all around the leviathan.
Best method to farm keys is roaming inside the royal gardens killing enemies and opening chests whenever one spawns.
Another good method is doing nightmare containments which you can open 3 chests at the end (the default chest on completion and 2 around the area)

Method 2: Focusing Opulent weapons


You can get focus Opulant weapons in the H.E.L.M and get the Drang (BAROQUE)
Again the Incandicent Roll is rng but you can directly focus a Drang (BAROQUE) instead of opening a chests and getting it randomly among other Opulant weapons.
You will need to upgrade the crown of sorrow and unlock the ability to focus Opulant weapons first.
You will also need some Opulant Umbral Energy and an Opulant engram in order to focus an opulant weapon

Method 3: Die


Some times rng won't give you the Drang (BAROQUE) with the desired roll. I got mine after 14 failed Drangs. :)))
